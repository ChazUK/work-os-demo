Partnership with the NRLA

Portfolio is proudly developed in partnership with the National Residential Landlords Association (NRLA), the UK's leading membership organization for private residential landlords.

Built by Landlords, for Landlords

Our platform is shaped by the insights and feedback of thousands of NRLA members, ensuring that every feature addresses real-world needs and challenges faced by landlords in the UK market.

Compliance-First Approach

With the NRLA's expertise in regulatory matters, Portfolio stays ahead of legislative changes, helping landlords maintain compliance with evolving UK property laws and safety requirements.

Trusted Resources

NRLA members benefit from integrated access to expert guidance, legal templates, and best practice resources directly within the Portfolio platform, ensuring professional property management.
